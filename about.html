Our Story

The DSATM Placement Cell is the cornerstone of career development at Dayananda Sagar Academy of Technology & Management. Established in 2010, we have successfully placed over 1,200 students in top-tier companies across IT, core engineering, and other sectors.

Our team is dedicated to preparing students for the industry through comprehensive training programs, including resume workshops, mock interviews, and technical skill development. We partner with over 150 recruiters, ensuring diverse opportunities for our students.

With a 95% placement rate, our personalized approach ensures that every student is equipped for long-term career success. We don't just facilitate placementsâ€”we build careers.
